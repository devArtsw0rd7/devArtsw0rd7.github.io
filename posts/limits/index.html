Limits

September 14, 2021

Lord,

I was awoken from a dark dream to the smell of Chai tea and whispers… Aurora and Elanor getting up to pray. After the dream, this was a welcome reality.

I am running up against my limits as regards self-discipline, it seems. My desire to get up early and run, exercise, etc. is utterly gone. I know running later will be more challenging, but I can’t make myself do it.

‘Spiritual confusion can only be conquered through obedience.’

There’s a thought. A thought I’ve had before, maybe even read before. Not tried, though, really. No, unfortunately, I am easily blown and tossed around by my own evil and selfish desires.

Lord, this morning I attempt to turn from those desires to you. Pride Inadequacy Lust Fear

I lay them aside, and ask for your forgiveness. I put myself forward into your presence, like one initially unwillingly volunteered. I bow now, obedient, desirous to explore your depths.

Do you smile? Do you know better? How can you be so loving and be so ancient? You are not like us.

Lord, help me prepare to say the words that need to be heard this week. Give me confidence and purpose. Even now the whispers at the back of my head - ‘you’re not good enough’ - I ignore them and look to you, Jesus.

Lord, help me with my finances, with our spending… help me to rightly order my life around you.

Thank you for music. It helps.
